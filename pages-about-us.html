About Us

Established in the year 2000, Das Tours And Travel is one of India’s best tour and travel agency in Kolkata, West Bengal. We are always at the service of our valued associates and customers in providing the most professional and personalized services as an reliable Tour Operator.

We completed 18 successful years in the travel industry specializing in offering travel services – both Domestic and International holiday packages, to a wide spectrum of satisfied and repeat guests and we are proud to be accredited with apex bodies and prestigious travel associations as best tour operators in kolkata. Our ethos – From the conception of DAS Tours And Travel till date , remains the same “Customer Service with a Smile, Patience and Good Communications”! We believe that Success is measured by the number of satisfied customers and that is always been our endeavor.

What makes us different ?
That said, DAS Tours And Travels is approved as Domestic Tour Operator by Ministry of Tourism, Government of India. We offer best prices, at any time, at Das Tours And Travels we believe that each travel plan has to be the best, From the most elementary to the most consequential aspects of travel are looked into with utmost care. With an enthusiastic and an experienced team, We strive to do our best, and are committed to ensuring a dream travel to all our guests..
